The Truth

The Painful Truth About Book Marketing
That Nobody Tells Authors

Publishing a book is not the same as marketing a book.

The best-selling books in the world are not always the best-written. They are the best-marketed. The difference between a book that sells ten copies and one that sells ten thousand is almost always the quality of the marketing behind it.

Invisible Books

Thousands of brilliant books are published daily and never found by the readers who would love them — simply because nobody told those readers the book existed.

No Strategy

Most authors publish and wait for sales that never come. Without a strategic marketing plan, even extraordinary books struggle to find their audience in the noise.

Wrong Positioning

Books not correctly positioned with the right keywords and categories are essentially invisible to Amazon's algorithm and the millions of readers who use it every day.
